About Me

I'm Monica and I live in Indiana, US. I love Music and Joan Jett is one of my favorite artists. I have listened to her for most of my life and her music has moved me deeply. I created this page as part of a SheCodes Responsive project to develop my coding skills.

About this website

As stated, before I created this page for a SheCodes Responsive project. I chose music for my project because music has always been a part of my life. I have many favorite artists, but it was easy to pick the Great Joan Jett. Her music moves me in many ways. She is a trailblazer for women in rock. I feel sometimes she is unfairly overlooked when it comes to what she has done and is still doing for the industry. Women would not be where we are in music, if it were not for Joan. Thank you for taking the time to read this and learn more about one of my idols and favorite artists.
